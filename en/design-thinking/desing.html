Design Thinking Workshop

"Design thinking" is presented as a methodology to develop innovation focused on people, offering a lens through which you can observe the challenges, detect needs and, finally, solve them. In this section we explain in detail the stages of Design Thinking:
EMPATIZING, DEFINING, IDEATING, PROTOTYPING AND TESTING.
