Gantt Chart - Sketch/Functional Decomposition

In this page you can see the use of the techniques we use to start implementing our PHOTOVOLTAIC ENERGY project based on the research carried out by the members of the group.

Gantt Chart

The Gantt chart is a graphical tool whose objective is to expose the expected dedication time for different tasks or activities over a given total time; in this case we will use it to start up the project of the SOLAR BACKPACK.

Archive - Gantt Chart

Initial Draft

The sketch represents the first concrete step of the work, that is, the first materialization in physics. In this case the first prototype design of the possible model and location of the solar panels in the backpack.

Document - Initial Draft

Functional Decomposition

Functional decomposition refers broadly to the process of solving a functional relation in its constituent parts, in such a way that the original function can be reconstructed from the parts according to their composition.
In the project was applied to recognize the components and know about each of them, as well as its operation.
